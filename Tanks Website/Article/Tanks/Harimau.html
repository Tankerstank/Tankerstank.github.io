Kaplan MT / Harimau

Kaplan MT or Harimau is a lightweight tank jointly developed by Turkish manufacturer FNSS and Indonesian manufacturer Pindad. The development program name for the tank is Modern Medium Weight Tank (MMWT). The tank itself is called Kaplan MT by Turkey and Harimau by Indonesia, both meaning "tiger".

The goverments of Indonesia and Turkey first agreed in May of 2015 to jointly develop the MMWT for the Indonesian Army at a reported cost of 30 million US dollars. The development of the tank took around 6 years with the development finishing in March of 2022
